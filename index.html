MindSnap Privacy Policy

Simple, honest, and human-readable. This policy explains how MindSnap handles journal, goal, notification, iCloud, and Apple Health data.

🔒

Your Data Belongs to You

MindSnap is designed to keep your personal journal and goals private. We do not sell your data, track you across apps, or use third-party advertising analytics.

✅ No Account Needed ☁️ Private iCloud Sync 🚫 No Third-Party Tracking
📋

What We Collect

❌ We Do Not Collect for Ourselves

  • ✗Your name or email unless you contact us directly
  • ✗Your precise location
  • ✗Device identifiers for tracking
  • ✗Third-party analytics
  • ✗Advertising tracking data
  • ✗Health data for advertising or sale

✅ Stored by the App for Features

  • ✓Journal entries you write
  • ✓Mood analysis results created on device
  • ✓Goals, reminders, progress, and streaks
  • ✓App preferences
  • ✓Selected Apple Health totals if you enable Health sync
💾

How Your Data Is Stored

  • ✓MindSnap stores app data locally on your iPhone using iOS app storage.
  • ✓If iCloud is available and enabled, journal and goal data may sync through Apple's private CloudKit service to your iCloud account.
  • ✓We do not run a separate server that can read your journal or goal data.
  • ✓Your data is protected by Apple's iOS security, your device passcode, and Face ID or Touch ID where enabled.
  • ✓Deleting the app removes local app data from your device. Data already synced to iCloud may remain in iCloud according to Apple's iCloud settings and sync behavior.
❤️

Apple Health

Apple Health sync is optional. MindSnap asks for permission before reading any Health data. You can approve or deny each Health data type in iOS.

  • ✓MindSnap may read selected daily totals such as steps, walking/running distance, cycling distance, swimming distance, exercise minutes, and water.
  • ✓Health data is used only to update matching goals so you do not need to enter everything manually.
  • ✓MindSnap does not write data to Apple Health.
  • ✓MindSnap does not sell Health data, use it for advertising, or share it with third-party analytics services.
  • ✓You can turn Health access off anytime in MindSnap Settings or iOS Settings.
🧠

Mood Analysis and Speech

  • ✓MindSnap analyzes journal mood on device to help you understand your emotions.
  • ✓Your journal text is not sent to third-party analytics or advertising services.
  • ✓If speech features are used, MindSnap requires Apple's on-device speech recognition where available.
🔔

Notifications

MindSnap uses local notifications for reminders you create. High priority reminders may use Apple's Time Sensitive notification behavior when available and permitted by iOS.

Important: MindSnap does not promise to bypass Do Not Disturb or Focus modes. Whether a Time Sensitive notification appears or plays sound depends on your iPhone notification and Focus settings.

✋

Your Rights and Control

  • 🗑️
    Delete local data anytime using MindSnap settings where available, or by deleting the app.
  • ☁️
    Manage iCloud using your iPhone's Apple ID and iCloud settings.
  • ❤️
    Manage Health permissions anytime in iOS Settings.
  • 🔔

  • 🔒
    Protect your journal with Face ID or Touch ID where enabled.
