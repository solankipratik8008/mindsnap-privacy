Storage, iCloud, and CloudKit

  • ✓MindSnap stores app data locally on your iPhone using iOS app storage.
  • ✓If iCloud is available and enabled on your device, your journal and goal data may sync to your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service.
  • ✓MindSnap does not operate a separate server that can read your journal or goals.
  • ✓Data synced through iCloud is controlled by your Apple ID, iCloud settings, and Apple's iCloud security model.
  • ✓Deleting the app removes local app data from the device. Data already synced to iCloud may remain in iCloud according to Apple's iCloud behavior and your device settings.
❤️

Apple Health

Apple Health sync is optional. MindSnap asks for permission before reading Health data. You can approve or deny each Health data type in iOS.

  • ✓MindSnap may read selected daily totals such as steps, walking/running distance, cycling distance, swimming distance, exercise minutes, and water.
  • ✓Health data is used only to update matching goal progress, including decimal distance values such as kilometers or miles.
  • ✓If Health access is off or unavailable, manual progress entry still works.
  • ✓MindSnap does not write data to Apple Health.
  • ✓MindSnap does not sell, share, or use Health data for advertising or third-party analytics.
  • ✓You can disable Health access anytime in MindSnap Settings or in iOS Settings.

Health information note: MindSnap is a habit and journaling tool. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent medical conditions, and it should not be used to make medical decisions.
