MindSnap Privacy Policy

Simple, honest, and human-readable. No legal jargon.

🔒

Your Journal is 100% Private

MindSnap was built from the ground up with one rule: your thoughts stay on your device, always.

✅ No Account Needed 📱 On-Device Only 🚫 Zero Data Collection
📋

What We Collect

❌ We Do NOT Collect

  • ✗ Your journal entries
  • ✗ Your name or email
  • ✗ Your location
  • ✗ Device identifiers
  • ✗ Usage analytics
  • ✗ Crash reports

✅ Stored on YOUR Device

  • ✓ Journal entries you write
  • ✓ Mood analysis results
  • ✓ App preferences
  • ✓ Streak counter
💾

How Data Is Stored

  • ✓ All data is stored using Apple's SwiftData in an encrypted database on your iPhone.
  • ✓ Protected by iOS encryption and your device passcode or Face ID.
  • ✓ Deleting the app permanently removes all data from your device.
  • ✓ iCloud backup follows your own iOS backup settings — we don't control this.
🤖

AI & Third Party Services

  • ✓ Mood analysis uses Apple's on-device NaturalLanguage framework. Your text never leaves your device.
  • ✓ We use ZERO third-party SDKs — no Firebase, no Google Analytics, no Mixpanel, no ads.
  • ✓ Apple App Store Privacy Label: Data Not Collected
✋

Your Rights & Control

  • 🗑️
    Delete all data anytime via Settings → Clear All Journal Data
  • 🔒
    Protect your journal with Face ID or Touch ID
  • 📱
    Delete the app to permanently remove every piece of data
  • ✈️
    Works offline — no internet connection ever required
